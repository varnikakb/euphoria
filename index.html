We provide nothingness

Euphoria Charities is a non-profit organization that works to give underprivileged people around the world a chance at a better life. Our goal is to help people suffering from large problems around the world, mainly Covid-19, poverty/hunger, and refugees. We realized that the toys and other unnecessary items we donate to charity aren’t helping people who are poor and without money, are living in poverty, or have a sick family member. Those toys might make a kid happy, but it won’t solve their problems. So, instead of donating relatively useless items, we decided to sell our unused or gently used toys and other things we don’t use to earn money to donate. We chose multiple trusted charities to support, and every cent of our proceeds go to them.

Why Should You Buy From Us?

You may wonder why you should buy from Euphoria Charities when you can just go to your local store and buy the same items. Well, one of the main reasons you should buy from us is because the money you pay for your item(s) goes to a good cause and to people who are less privileged than us. Another important reason we are a good choice to buy from us is that though some of our products may be pre-owned or gently used, they are still in good condition and at a lower price than they would be at the store. The majority of these items were used only once or never, then lived at the back of a shelf in our homes for years. Surely this happens in many households: a kid gets a present, opens it, never plays with it, and gives it away about four years later. Instead of donating a toy that is useless to someone who is living in poverty, selling that toy for money to donate is more helpful.

World Hunger: Rise Against Hunger

https://www.riseagainsthunger.org/

To support the end of hungry people in the world, one of the charities we support and donate our proceeds to is Rise Against Hunger. They have packaged over 500 thousand meals and sent them to over 1.8 million people in 2019. They have given to people in Italy, India, Malaysia, South Africa, the Philippines, Mexico, and more. To support this cause, donate to Rise Against Hunger before checkout when buying your product. If you would like to go a bit above and beyond, you can donate extra to this charity by selecting the donate extra option when buying an item, or going to the donate now bar at the top of our website.
